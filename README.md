## The Problem with Search Today

Every search engine treats you the same. A PhD researcher and a curious 12-year-old get identical results for the same query. You are handed 10 blue links and left to do all the hard cognitive work yourself вҖ” filtering noise, synthesizing contradictions, reading between the lines.

**Socratic Search was built to fix that.**

Instead of asking *what do you want to know?*, it first asks *who are you?* It opens a Socratic dialogue to map your background, mental model, and purpose вҖ” then it deploys the full power of **Gemini 2.5 Pro** on live web data to synthesize a **Discovery Brief** that speaks directly to *you*, in *your* language, at *your* level.

This is not a better search engine. It is a **personalized intelligence agency** вҖ” one that works exclusively for you.

### Phase 1 вҖ” Socratic Dialogue

Before any search is triggered, the app engages you in a brief but meaningful conversation. It asks questions like:

- What is your background or expertise level on this topic?
- What are you trying to accomplish with this information?
- How do you prefer to receive information вҖ” through stories, data, frameworks, or examples?
- What do you already know, and what is the gap you are trying to fill?

This is not filler. Your answers directly shape every sentence of the output that follows.

### Phase 2 вҖ” Intelligent Synthesis

With your profile established, Gemini 2.5 Pro takes over. It does not just search вҖ” it synthesizes. It queries the live web, cross-references sources, weighs credibility, identifies patterns, and assembles the most relevant information as though a senior research analyst had done it by hand вҖ” with full awareness of who you are.

### Phase 3 вҖ” The Discovery Brief

The final output is not a list of links. It is a **structured, narrative-driven brief** вҖ” formatted to your learning style, calibrated to your expertise level, and built around your specific goals. Think of it as a custom-written article, assembled about exactly what you asked, written specifically for you.

## The Socratic Method

The name is not decorative. The app's dialogue engine is directly inspired by **Socratic questioning** вҖ” the ancient Greek method of inquiry used by Socrates to expose assumptions, reveal gaps, and sharpen understanding through guided conversation.

The core principle: before you can answer a question well, you must first understand who is asking it and why.

Socratic Search embodies this in software. Rather than accepting a surface-level query and returning generic results, it asks:

> *"What do I not yet know about this person that I need to know in order to truly serve them?"*

And then it finds out вҖ” briefly, conversationally, respectfully.

**The contrast with traditional search:**

| Dimension | Traditional Search | Socratic Search |
|---|---|---|
| **Input** | A single query string | A profile built through dialogue |
| **Output** | A ranked list of links | A synthesized, personalized brief |
| **Personalization** | None вҖ” identical for all users | Deep вҖ” unique for every user |
| **Interpretation** | You filter and interpret results | The AI interprets results for you |
| **Expertise calibration** | None | Calibrated to your stated level |
| **Cognitive load** | High вҖ” you connect the dots | Low вҖ” the dots are connected for you |
| **Depth** | As deep as you're willing to dig | Automatically pitched to your needs |
| **Effort** | Multiple searches, many tabs | One conversation, one complete brief |

The dialogue is short вҖ” typically three to five questions. But the downstream difference in output quality is profound. Two people asking the exact same question in the exact same words will receive completely different briefs if they have different backgrounds and goals.

That is the entire point.

## What is a Discovery Brief?

A Discovery Brief is the final output of Socratic Search. It is not a list of links, not a generic summary, and not a copy-paste from Wikipedia. It is a **bespoke research document**, assembled live, specifically for you.

**Anatomy of a Discovery Brief:**

**1. Context Setting**
Why this topic matters right now, where it sits in the broader landscape, and what makes it worth understanding.

**2. Core Findings**
The most important, relevant, and current information drawn from live web sources вҖ” synthesized, not listed.

**3. Layered Depth**
Details scaled precisely to your expertise. A beginner receives analogies and concrete examples. An expert receives nuance, edge cases, and technical precision. The same underlying facts, rendered differently.

**4. Connections**
How this topic connects to adjacent ideas, fields, or concerns that you вҖ” given your stated profile вҖ” are likely to care about.

**5. Key Takeaways**
Distilled, actionable insights you can walk away with immediately. No homework required.

**6. Further Exploration**
Curated directions for going deeper, if the brief has sparked your curiosity. Not a list of links вҖ” a map of the terrain ahead.

The result reads less like a search results page and more like a report that a knowledgeable, thoughtful colleague wrote specifically for your situation, on the day you asked.

## FAQ

**Is this free to use?**
Yes. The app is fully open source. The only cost is your Gemini API usage вҖ” and the free tier from Google AI Studio is more than enough for personal use.

**What happens when I hit the API quota limit?**
Simply generate a new free key at [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey), update your `.env.local` file, and continue. It takes about 30 seconds.

**Does it work on mobile?**
It functions on mobile, but the experience is optimized for a wide-screen, fullscreen desktop environment. A mobile-first layout is on the roadmap.

**Why does it recommend fullscreen mode?**
The Discovery Brief is designed to be read like a focused document вҖ” not skimmed like a results page. Fullscreen removes the browser chrome and gives the content the space and atmosphere it needs to land properly.

**Why Gemini 2.5 Pro specifically?**
Gemini 2.5 Pro has exceptional live web grounding, strong multi-step reasoning for synthesis tasks, and a generous free tier вҖ” making it the right choice for a tool that depends heavily on both real-time data access and high-quality language generation.

**Can I use a different AI model?**
The current codebase is built around the Gemini API. Contributions that add clean, optional support for other models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral) are welcome.

**Is my data stored anywhere?**
No. The app runs entirely in your browser and communicates directly with the Gemini API. No conversation data, no search queries, and no user information is stored by this application on any external server.

**How long does the Socratic dialogue take?**
Usually under two minutes. It is deliberately short вҖ” three to five questions вҖ” to respect your time while gathering enough context to meaningfully personalize the brief.

**Can I skip the dialogue and search directly?**
The dialogue is the mechanism that makes the brief valuable. Skipping it would reduce the output to a generic summary вҖ” something any standard AI tool already provides. Lean into the questions; the quality difference is significant.
